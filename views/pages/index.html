Wiki Books

Augmenting the Facebook Free Internet Project

About

Wiki books gives people in third world countries access to important websites, like wikipedia. We accomplish this through the Facebook Internet Project, which gives citizens of certain countries limited free internet access, generally only to Facebook.com

Wiki Books is a chat bot on Facebook that users can interact with to view information on other websites. Through this, we hope to enhance the lives of those without full internet.
